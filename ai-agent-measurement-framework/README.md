# AI Agent Measurement Framework

## Overview

Organizations are rapidly deploying AI agents, yet most struggle to answer a fundamental question:

**Are these agents creating measurable business value?**

This project introduces a practical measurement framework for evaluating AI agents across five dimensions:

1. Adoption
2. Effectiveness
3. Productivity
4. Incrementality
5. Financial Impact

The framework combines analytics, experimentation, and business measurement principles commonly used in Marketing Science, Product Analytics, and Measurement Science.

---

## Problem Statement

Many organizations measure AI success using simple usage metrics:

* Number of users
* Number of prompts
* Number of tasks completed

These metrics do not demonstrate business impact.

This framework provides a methodology to determine whether AI agents:

* Improve outcomes
* Increase productivity
* Generate incremental value
* Deliver positive ROI

---

## Framework

### Adoption

Measures whether users engage with the AI system.

### Effectiveness

Measures output quality and task success.

### Productivity

Measures time savings and throughput improvements.

### Incrementality

Measures causal impact using experimentation methods.

### Financial Impact

Measures ROI and business outcomes.

---

## Methodology

The project simulates organizational AI-agent usage data and evaluates performance using:

* Descriptive Analytics
* A/B Testing
* Difference-in-Differences
* Incrementality Analysis
* ROI Modeling

---

## Example Business Questions

* Are employees completing tasks faster with AI?
* Does AI improve quality?
* What is the incremental value of AI adoption?
* Which teams benefit most from AI?
* Should deployment be expanded?
